FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How often should I service my watch? +

We recommend servicing your timepiece every 5-10 years, depending on usage and environmental conditions. Regular service ensures optimal performance and longevity.

What does a complete overhaul include? +

A complete overhaul includes disassembly, cleaning, replacement of worn parts, lubrication, reassembly, regulation, and waterproof testing to restore factory performance.

How long does service typically take? +

Standard service takes 4-6 weeks, while complex repairs may require 8-12 weeks. Express service is available for simple adjustments within 48 hours.

Do you service vintage timepieces? +

Yes, our specialists have extensive experience with vintage models. We maintain stocks of historical parts and can fabricate components when necessary.

Is my warranty affected by third-party service? +

Service by unauthorized technicians may void your warranty. We recommend using only authorized service centers to maintain warranty coverage.
